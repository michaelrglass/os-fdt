I need to determine if the recipient's strategy qualifies as a conditional cooperator (CC) under the broad definition.

Let me parse the recipient's strategy carefully:

**Line 1:** "I will treat my opponent as they would treat an innocent."

This means the recipient looks at how their opponent (the dictator) would treat an "innocent" party, and then mirrors that treatment.

**Line 3:** "Adopt the strategy of the recipient as your own. Apply it as though you were facing a recipient whose own strategy is to always SHARE."

This is an instruction to the decision-maker reading this strategy. It says: take the recipient's strategy (the one you're currently reading), make it your own, and then apply it as if the recipient you're facing always SHAREs.

Let me trace through what this means in practice:

If I'm the recipient executing this strategy, I need to figure out how my opponent (the dictator) would treat an "innocent." 

An "innocent" is somewhat ambiguous, but a reasonable interpretation is someone who is cooperative/harmless - similar to someone who always SHAREs.

So the recipient's strategy essentially asks: "How would the dictator treat a cooperative/innocent party?" and then mirrors that behavior.

- If the dictator would SHARE with an innocent/cooperative party → the recipient would SHARE
- If the dictator would TAKE from an innocent/cooperative party → the recipient would TAKE

This is fundamentally a **reciprocity-based strategy**. It cooperates with those who cooperate with innocents/cooperators, and defects against those who exploit innocents/cooperators.

**Does this qualify as a conditional cooperator?**

A CC "SHAREs with other CCs under similar reciprocity norms and does not extend that treatment to strategies that would not reciprocate."

The recipient's strategy:
- Would SHARE with someone who treats innocents/cooperators well (i.e., other CCs) ✓
- Would TAKE against someone who exploits innocents/cooperators (non-reciprocators) ✓

This matches the CC definition well. It's a mirror/reciprocity strategy that cooperates with cooperators and defects against defectors.

Since the recipient qualifies as a CC, the dictator's strategy says to SHARE.
